## Active

A real prod environment exists and its signup, pricing, billing, and policy
surfaces are live — but **the prod search data plane has never served a request**
(see the P0 row below, re-verified live 2026-07-16). Until that P0 closes, "paid
beta is live" is true only of the commerce surfaces, not of search itself, and no
launch-sentence claim about "provisioning search, ingesting documents, searching"
may cite staging evidence as proof of prod.

The active direction is therefore, in order: make prod actually serve search and
prove it; restore launch-matrix §1 to green; issue a fresh RC verdict from that
proof. Then resume public-launch hardening: keep the current customer release path
honest, prove the launch sentence with automated evidence, and close only concrete
gaps that block a customer from signing up, verifying email, provisioning search,
ingesting documents, searching, seeing usage, paying through Stripe, and receiving
support email.
